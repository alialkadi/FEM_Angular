Privacy Policy

Fenestration Services is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Request an estimate or service
  • Contact us through our website, email, or phone
  • Make a payment or complete a service agreement

This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Address, email, and phone number
  • Details related to your property or service request
  • Payment information processed securely through verified systems

We may also collect non-identifiable data through analytics tools, such as browser type, device information, IP address, general location, and website usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • Provide, schedule, and manage window and door services
  • Communicate estimates, invoices, and service updates
  • Process secure payments
  • Improve customer experience and website functionality
  • Send service-related notices or limited promotional updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Trusted third-party partners such as payment processors, IT providers, or subcontractors
  • Legal or regulatory authorities if required by law

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and security obligations consistent with Canadian privacy laws.

4. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no online transmission or storage system is completely secure; users share data at their own discretion.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable Canadian privacy legislation, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to retention obligations

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance browsing experience
  • Monitor website performance
  • Customize content and offers

You can adjust or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to other websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect business changes or legal updates. The latest version will always be posted on our website with an updated effective date.
